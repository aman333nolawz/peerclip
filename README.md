# Peerclip
Share your clipboard and files between your devices using peer-to-peer technology.
Just go to https://nolawz.xyz/peerclip and you can start sharing your clipboard and files between your devices.

> [!WARNING]
> The frontent is pure AI slop since I am not that good at designing. The p2p stuff was implemented by me though

## Why?
I just wanted a service so that i can share my clipboard and files between my devices. So I made this.

Since I was interested in p2p for a while now, I decided this would be the best project to learn and experiment with it.

## Techstack
- Vite + Svelte (frontend)
- Peerjs (p2p)
